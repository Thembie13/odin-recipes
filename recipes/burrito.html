Burrito

Description

This is a great and filling mea. It is a saucy and savory meal, that is filled with delicious fillings.

This burrito can be done in many variations, and flavored in wonderful ways.

It is a warm and hearty meal. So enjoy!

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can of black or pinto beans
  • 1 large tomato, coarsely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons of lime juice, or apple cider vinegar
  • Burrito-size flour tortillas
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the beef with onions, unitl the meat is cooked
  2. While cooking the beef, add seasoning such as smoked paprika, salt, pepper, and/or cumin.
  3. Add beans and tomato to the pan, make sure to include the liquid. Only turn off the heat whne the liquid has evaporated, and the mixture starts to sizzle.
  4. Arrange the tortillas,add the filling and fold the tortilla
  5. When ready to eat, you can put the burrito in a skillet, until it is golden brown
