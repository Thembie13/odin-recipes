Samosa

Description

This is a recipe of one of my favorite appetizers.

This is an appetizer that can be filled with the most wonderful spice combination.

If you are looking forgood party/game food, look no farther than this recipe

Ingrediants

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • 1 pack of ground beef
  • 1 medium red onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper, curry, thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander (optional)
  • 1 cup of veggies such as peas, carrots, or potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
  • Goya Empanada Dough Discos

Steps:

  1. In a large pan, fry onions, and when cooked, add ground beef and cook till brown
  2. In the same pan, add the necessary spices, and when cooked, add the veggies and cook till ready
  3. Take a disco, and use a rolling pin to roll it. Add a spoonfull of the cooked food, which is in larget plan, and fold the dough. Do this until you run out of dough or filling
  4. In a large, deep pan, heat the oul to medium high heat. Once the oil is heated, place 4 or more uncooked samosas in the pan. Fry for 3-5 minutes, until samosa is light, golden brown
