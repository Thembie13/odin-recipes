Tiramisu

Description

This is a recipe about my favorite, and one of the best desserts to exist: Tiramisu.

It is a rich and cream dessert that gets better, the longer it sits.

Tiramisu is incredibly easy to make, as well as rich and creamy to taste. Enjoy!

Ingrediants

  • Ladyfingers
  • Coffee
  • Rum(optional)
  • Mascarpone Cheese
  • Egg yolks
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Combine coffee, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and rum if using
  2. First, beat heavy cream and sugar until stiff peaks form. Secondly, whip egg yolks and suagr until it is pale yellow and fluffy. Lastly, fold the mascarpone cheese into the egg yolk mix, and then finish by folding in the whipped cream until there is no streaks remaining.
  3. Now it is time to assemble the tiramisu. The ladyfingers are first soaked, and then placed in the dish. Then another layer of mascarpone filling is put on top and spread into and even layer. Continue adding layers of ladyfingers and then filling.
  4. The last thing needed to complete the bake, is to dust the to dust the top with cocoa powder and let it sit in the fridge for 4 hours.
